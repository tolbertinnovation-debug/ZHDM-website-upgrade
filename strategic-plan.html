Executive Summary

Building a Lasting Legacy

This Strategic Plan outlines ZHDMI's commitment to growth, impact, and stewardship. We aim to transition from local community outreach to a nationwide movement of healing and empowerment.

2025

Foundation & Strengthening

Strengthening administrative governance, formalizing community partnerships in Johnsonville, and launching our first unified health and education pilot programs.

2026

Expanding Montserrado Outreach

Scaling health awareness drives and scholarship programs to cover at least five new districts within Montserrado County, reaching over 5,000 families.

2027

Regional Prayer Centers

Establishing regional prayer and deliverance hubs in three neighboring counties, training local intercessors and community leaders to lead spiritual restoration.

2028

Sustainable Infrastructure

Breaking ground on the ZHDMI National Worship & Vocational Center — a permanent facility for spiritual gatherings and tuition-free skills training for youth.

2029

National Impact & Global Partnership

Full scale-up of ministry programs across Liberia and the formalization of international missions, sharing the ZHDMI model of holistic restoration with the world.

Strategic Pillars

The Four Drives

🕊️

Spiritual Restoration

Prioritizing healing and deliverance through weekly services, all-night vigils, and the planting of vibrant prayer centers.

🎓

Human Development

Investing in the next generation through scholarships, school supply drives, and youth leadership mentorship programs.

🏥

Community Wellness

Providing essential healthcare awareness and screenings to underserved communities, ensuring physical healing alongside spiritual.

⚖️

Operational Stewardship

Maintaining 100% transparency in financial reporting and building a robust administrative structure to support long-term growth.
