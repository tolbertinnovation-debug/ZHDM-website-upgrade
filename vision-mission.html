Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Values

Guiding our commitment to spiritual restoration and socio-economic transformation in the Republic of Liberia.

Proclaiming Christ

Our Mission

To proclaim the healing and deliverance power of Jesus Christ across Liberia, establishing prayer centers that foster spiritual growth while delivering impactful humanitarian programs in education, health awareness, and community development.

Envisioning Wholeness

Our Vision

A spiritually liberated and socio-economically vibrant Liberia, where the Gospel of Christ restores hope, quality education empowers the youth, and compassionate healthcare reaches every community.

Core Values

The Principles That Define Us

These pillars represent our non-negotiable commitment to God and the people of Liberia.

Biblical Integrity

Our ministry is built solely on the Holy Scriptures. We are committed to teaching truth without compromise and governing ZHDMI with biblical ethics.

Compassionate Service

Following the example of Jesus, we reach out to the "least of these." We prioritize serving the vulnerable in underserved regions of Montserrado County.

Radical Transparency

We believe in absolute accountability. Every gift and resource is managed with stewardship, ensuring it directly impacts the communities we serve.

Sustainable Empowerment

We don't just provide temporary aid; we equip Liberians with spiritual and practical tools—through education and skills training—for lasting self-reliance.

Communal Unity

We foster a spirit of togetherness and partnership, working with churches and government to uplift the Liberian nation through collective action.
