Ministry 02: Education

Educational Programs

Life-skills training, literacy, mentorship, and educational support that open doors of opportunity and promote lasting self-sufficiency.

What We Offer

Programs That Open Doors

📚

Adult Literacy

Reading, writing, and numeracy classes for adults returning to learning at any stage of life.

🔧

Vocational Training

Hands-on trade skills, tailoring, carpentry, agriculture, and technology, taught by practitioners.

🧑‍🏫

Youth Mentorship

Tutoring, after-school support, and one-on-one mentoring for children, youth & families.

💰

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, and stewardship courses that build stable households and futures.
