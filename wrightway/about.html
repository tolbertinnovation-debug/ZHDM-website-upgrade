About Us

Founded on Faith. Built for Restoration.

the wRightWay Global Ministries, Inc. exists to meet both practical and spiritual needs while creating opportunities for lasting transformation and hope.

Our Story

A Ministry Born of Compassion

the wRightWay Global Ministries, Inc. is a faith-based nonprofit organization committed to transforming lives and strengthening communities through the love, power, and truth of Jesus Christ.

The ministry serves homeless, impoverished, vulnerable, and underserved individuals and families by providing compassionate outreach, restoration services, education, spiritual development, and community empowerment initiatives. Founded on biblical principles of compassion, integrity, service, and restoration, the organization exists to meet both practical and spiritual needs while creating opportunities for lasting transformation and hope.

Religion that God our Father accepts as pure and faultless is this: to look after orphans and widows in their distress.

— James 1:27

Mission, Vision & Core Values

🎯

Our Mission

The wRightWay Global Ministries exists to restore hope, empower lives, and strengthen communities through Christ-centered outreach, housing, education, spiritual development, and global missions.

👓

Our Vision

To see lives, families, and communities transformed worldwide through the love, truth, and power of Jesus Christ by providing spiritual guidance, humanitarian support, education, housing, and opportunities for lasting restoration.

✝ Christ-Centered Service

Jesus is the foundation and motivation of everything we do.

💙 Compassion

We meet people in their need with the heart of Christ.

⚖️ Integrity

We act with honesty, accountability, and transparency.

💪 Empowerment

We equip people to rise into self-sufficiency and purpose.

⭐ Excellence

We pursue the highest standard in service and stewardship.

🤝 Unity

We labor together across churches, cultures, and nations.

🕊 Restoration

We believe every life can be rebuilt with dignity and hope.

Our Pillars

Five Organizational Pillars

01
🥕

Outreach & Compassion Services

Food assistance, clothing, hygiene resources, crisis support, community outreach, and care initiatives for individuals and families facing hardship.

02
🏠

Housing & Restoration

Transitional housing, recovery support, family stabilization, and programs that help individuals rebuild their lives with dignity and purpose.

03
📚

Education & Empowerment

Life-skills training, mentorship, workforce readiness, educational support, financial literacy, and empowerment opportunities that promote self-sufficiency.

04
🙏

Spiritual Development & Evangelism

Biblical teaching, discipleship, prayer support, counseling, evangelism, leadership development, and spiritual growth opportunities.

05
🌍

Global Missions & Community Development

Partnering locally and globally to strengthen communities through humanitarian aid, outreach missions, development initiatives, and collaborative partnerships.

Who We Serve

Our Target Population

  • Low-income and underserved communities
  • Youth and young adults — children, youth & families
  • Seniors and vulnerable populations
  • Individuals in crisis or transition
