Community Development

Housing, food security, and health outreach that strengthen families and stabilize neighborhoods, meeting practical needs with the compassion of Christ.

Core Services

Strengthening Families, Stabilizing Neighborhoods

🏠

Housing Projects

Transitional housing, recovery support, and family stabilization that rebuild lives with dignity and purpose.

🥕

Food Programs

Food assistance, community pantries, and hot-meal outreach for individuals and families facing hardship.

🩺

Health Outreach

Health screenings, hygiene resources, and clinic partnerships serving vulnerable populations.

👪

Crisis Support

Clothing, emergency resources, and care initiatives for people in crisis or transition.

Our housing and restoration pillar supports transitional housing, recovery support, and family stabilization, pairing shelter with case management, spiritual care, and pathways into our education and career programs.
