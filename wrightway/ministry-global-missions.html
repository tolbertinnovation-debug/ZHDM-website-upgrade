Ministry 06: The Nations

Global Missions

Mission trips, humanitarian aid, and church planting, carrying the Gospel and practical help from Liberia and West Africa to the ends of the earth.

The Great Commission

Called to the Nations

Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit.

— Matthew 28:19

Our global missions pillar partners locally and internationally to strengthen communities through humanitarian aid, outreach missions, development initiatives, and collaborative partnerships, beginning in Liberia and West Africa and expanding wherever God opens doors.
