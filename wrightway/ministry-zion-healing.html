Ministry 01: Church & Worship

Zion Healing & Deliverance Ministry

Our founding church in Johnsonville, Montserrado County, Liberia, bringing healing, deliverance, and hope through the power of Jesus Christ.

The Mother Church

Where the Work Began

Zion Healing & Deliverance Ministries International (ZHDMI) is the founding congregation of the wRightWay Global Ministries family: a Christian church in Liberia devoted to worship, healing services, deliverance ministry, and compassionate care for the surrounding community.
