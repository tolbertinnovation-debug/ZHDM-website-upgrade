Ministry 05: Liberia

National Development Projects

Infrastructure, clean water, and agriculture initiatives in Liberia, sustainable development that lifts whole communities out of poverty.

Building Liberia

Development With Deep Roots

Working from our field office in Johnsonville, Montserrado County, we partner with local leaders on projects designed for long-term, community-owned impact.

💧

Clean Water

Well drilling, pump maintenance, and safe-water education for villages and school compounds.

🌾

Agriculture

Seed programs, farming cooperatives, and training that strengthen food security and income.

🏗️

Infrastructure

Community buildings, school repairs, and sanitation projects built with local labor.

💼

Economic Development

Market access, micro-enterprise, and trade training that keep value in the community.
