Who We Are

Bringing God's Love to Every Corner of the World

the wRightWay Global Ministries, Inc. is a faith-based nonprofit organization committed to transforming lives and strengthening communities through the love, power, and truth of Jesus Christ. We serve homeless, impoverished, vulnerable, and underserved individuals and families through compassionate outreach, restoration services, education, spiritual development, and community empowerment.

🕊️

Spiritual Development

Prayer, discipleship & biblical teaching that nurture lasting spiritual growth and leadership.

🏠

Housing & Restoration

Safe homes, recovery support, and family stabilization that rebuild lives with dignity and purpose.

📚

Education & Empowerment

Life skills, literacy, mentorship, and workforce readiness that promote self-sufficiency.

🤝

Global Outreach

Humanitarian aid, missions, and community development partnerships, locally and worldwide.
