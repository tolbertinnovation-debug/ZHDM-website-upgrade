Our Work

Ministry Programs

A technical overview of our spiritual and humanitarian initiatives in the Republic of Liberia.

Worship Discipleship Education Empowerment Health Counselling
⛪
Program 01

Church & Worship

Our primary spiritual engine, focused on establishing vibrant hubs of worship and corporate prayer. We believe that spiritual restoration is the foundation for all other forms of development.

Technical Implementation

  • Establishment of local prayer centers in underserved communities.
  • Training programs for worship leaders and liturgical coordinators.
  • Management of monthly all-night deliverance vigils.
  • Protocol development for community-based intercessory teams.
12+Weekly Hubs
4,000+Monthly Attendees
📖
Program 02

Evangelism & Discipleship

Moving beyond the sanctuary, this program focuses on the systematic spread of the Gospel and the structured maturation of believers through biblical education.

Strategic Framework

  • Deployment of national outreach crusades and revival seminars.
  • Structured 12-week discipleship curriculum for new converts.
  • Multimedia Gospel outreach reaching over 10,000 via digital platforms.
  • Leadership training for regional evangelism coordinators.
500+Disciples Trained
NationwideReach Status
🎓
Program 03

Education Support

A humanitarian initiative aimed at reducing the barriers to quality education for Liberia's youth, focusing on financial aid and logistical resources.

Logistical Protocol

  • Needs-based vetting process for annual scholarship disbursements.
  • Supply chain management for school kit distribution (books, uniforms).
  • Partnership framework with local Montserrado private and public schools.
  • Student performance monitoring and academic mentorship cycles.
1,500+Kits Distributed
150+Full Scholarships
💪
Program 04

Youth & Women Empowerment

Focusing on socio-economic independence, this program provides practical skills training to help vulnerable groups achieve self-reliance.

Operational Wings

  • Vocational modules including tailoring, soap making, and catering.
  • Entrepreneurial workshops focusing on small business management.
  • Youth leadership cohorts and career guidance counselling.
  • Economic empowerment support groups for women in Johnsonville.
300+Graduates Annually
45%Employment Rate
🌿
Program 05

Health Awareness

Integrating physical healing with spiritual care, we run community-wide health drives to address preventable diseases and hygiene standards.

Medical Coordination

  • Free screening protocols for malaria, hypertension, and diabetes.
  • WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene) education campaigns.
  • Collaboration with the Ministry of Health for regional outreach days.
  • Supply of essential over-the-counter medical aids to rural areas.
2,000+Screenings Held
25+Partner Clinics
🕊️
Program 06

Counselling & Spiritual Care

Providing holistic support for mental and spiritual health, specialized for those recovering from trauma or seeking life direction.

Care Methodologies

  • Confidential one-on-one spiritual counselling sessions.
  • Trauma-informed support frameworks for postwar reconciliation.
  • Family mediation and marital restoration protocols.
  • Grief support and spiritual mentorship for young orphans.
850+Sessions Logged
