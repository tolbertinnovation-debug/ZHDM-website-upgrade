Our Leadership

Guided by faith and professional stewardship to serve the communities of Liberia.

MW

Executive Director

Monica Wright

Monica Wright is the visionary founder and Executive Director of Zion Healing and Deliverance Ministries International.

With over a decade of dedication to spiritual ministry and community service in Liberia, she leads the organisation's efforts to integrate healing and deliverance with sustainable humanitarian programs. Under her leadership, ZHDMI has grown from a local prayer group to a registered NGO impacting thousands across Montserrado County.
