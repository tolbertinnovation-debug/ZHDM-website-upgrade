Who We Are

Bringing God's Love to Every Corner of Liberia

Zion Healing and Deliverance Ministries International (ZHDMI) is a Christian-based, non-profit NGO established in Liberia to serve communities through spiritual transformation, humanitarian outreach, and sustainable development.

✝

We believe the Gospel restores the whole person — spirit, soul, and body. We partner with churches, NGOs, and governments to bring lasting change across Montserrado County.

What We Do

Our Ministries & Programs

Every programme is rooted in Scripture and directed at meeting real needs in Liberian communities.

01
⛪

Church & Worship

Weekly worship, prayer meetings, and evangelistic outreach establishing prayer centres throughout Liberia.

02
📖

Evangelism & Discipleship

Preaching the Gospel through conferences, seminars, and multimedia outreach across the nation.

03
🎓

Education Support

Providing resources to underserved children and youth across Montserrado County and beyond.

04
💪

Youth & Women Empowerment

Promoting leadership, economic independence, and spiritual formation for young people and women.

05
🌿

Health Awareness

Community health campaigns bringing care to families lacking access to quality healthcare.

06
🕊️

Counselling & Spiritual Care

Spiritual guidance and counselling promoting healing, peace, and renewed purpose in Christ.
