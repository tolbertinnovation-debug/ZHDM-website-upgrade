Our Story

Founded on Faith, Built on Prayer

Zion Healing and Deliverance Ministries International (ZHDMI) was established as a Christian-based, non-profit NGO in the Republic of Liberia, rooted in a deep conviction that God desires to heal, deliver, and restore His people.

From its principal office in the Johnsonville Community, Montserrado County, ZHDMI reaches across Liberia with the Gospel, partnering with churches, NGOs, government institutions, and international organisations to bring lasting transformation.

Our Mission & Vision

✝

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel, heal the sick, deliver the oppressed, and serve communities through education, health, and sustainable development in Jesus' name.

🌟

Our Vision

A Liberia transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit — where every family knows Jesus, every child is educated, and every community thrives.

📖

Our Values

Faith, Integrity, Compassion, Transparency, Community, and unwavering commitment to the authority of Scripture in all we do.
